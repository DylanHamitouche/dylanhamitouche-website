Automating Actigraphy and REDCap Data Integration for Patient Summary Reports

I wrote code to automatize plotting of collected actigraphy data (sleep and physical activity) and REDCap data (questionnaires assessing symptoms) from patients enrolled in the Deep Phenotyping and Digitalization at Douglas (DeeP-DD). These visualizations were used to generate monthly summary reports presented to patients."
